# Main Mistakes to Avoid

* Do not merge raw historical tables directly with application table.
* Do not merge `bureau_balance` directly with application table.
* Do not treat repeated historical IDs as duplicate errors.
* Do not use `SK_ID_CURR`, `SK_ID_PREV`, `SK_ID_BUREAU` as model features.
* Do not fill all missing values too early.
* Do not drop high-missing columns too early.
* Do not do target-based feature engineering.
* Do not start modeling in this notebook.
